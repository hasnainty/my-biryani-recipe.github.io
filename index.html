BIRYANI RECIPIE

Ingredents:

  1. Chicken 2kg(small/medium pieces)
  2. Pakistan Star Rice 2kg
  3. Bay leaf-3/4 pieces
  4. Onion 4/5 pices (Big sized thinly sliced)
  5. Tomato puree-4/6(bid sized)
  6. Salt - Taste
  7. Oil 2 Cups
  8. Edible yellow color-1/2 Teaspoon
  9. Potatos - 4 pices(Medium cut)
  10. Garlic paste
  11. Biryani Masala

PROCEDURE:

  • Semi-Boil the Pakistan Star Rice with
    Bay Leaf and Salt to taste. Drain the water
    and keep them aside.
  • Marinate the Chicken with garlic paste,
    Masala and Salt keep it aside for marination.
  • Heat oil in the Pan and add the sliced
    onion to it and fry until light brown.
  • Add tomato puree to the oil and cook
    until the oil starts floating.Add chicken
    and fried potatos to the mixture.
  • Fill 1/6 pot with semi boiled pkistan
    star rice and layer the mixture about it.
    cover the mixture with remaning rive.
  • Take a small pan and heat oil in it.
    Add cinnamon pieces and bay leaf in it.
    put it cover the rice.Cook until rice and
    chicken are done
  • Now your Biryani is redy to serve.
